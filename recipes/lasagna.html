Lasagna Receipe

Description

This is what many describe as the best "World's Best Lasagna Receipe" On this site, I will describe step by step on how to make this recipe, along with all of the ingrediants and everything you need. I hope this can teach you how to make the best lasagna ever.

Ingrediants

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
